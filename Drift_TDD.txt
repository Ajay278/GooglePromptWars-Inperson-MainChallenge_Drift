# DRIFT — Technical Design Specification (MVP)

## Product Summary

DRIFT is an AI-powered behavioral coaching platform that prevents unhealthy digital habits before they become addictions.

Unlike screen-time trackers, DRIFT measures **Attention Drift**—the gradual movement from intentional behavior toward unconscious phone usage.

The AI does not maximize engagement.

It helps users eventually stop needing the app.

---

# Engineering Principles

## Core Rule

> AI explains behavior.
>
> AI never determines behavior.

Behavior analysis must always be deterministic and explainable.

LLMs are responsible for:

- Personalization
- Coaching
- Reflection
- Emotional rewards
- Natural language generation

Business logic never depends on AI.

---

# Product Goals

DRIFT should

- Detect Attention Drift
- Explain WHY it happened
- Recommend healthier replacements
- Reinforce future identity
- Encourage offline life
- Reduce dependence on DRIFT itself

# Core Components

## Behavior Engine

Responsible for

- Drift calculation
- Risk prediction
- Behavioral analysis

Outputs

- Drift Score
- Confidence
- Risk Level
- Contributing Factors

No AI involved.

---

## AI Orchestrator

Responsible for

- Coaching
- Weekly reflection
- Personalized explanations
- Emotional rewards

Never calculates Drift Score.

---

## Recommendation Engine

Ranks offline activities using

- Time
- Mood
- Energy
- Previous success
- Future Self goals
- Weather
- Distance (future)

---

## Safety Engine

Validates every AI response.

Rejects

- Shame
- Fear
- Diagnosis
- Manipulation
- Unsafe advice
- Time-inappropriate suggestions

# Engineering Philosophy

The Behavior Engine is the source of truth.

The AI is an adaptive behavioral coach layered on top.

The ultimate success metric is not engagement.

**The ultimate success of DRIFT is when users no longer need DRIFT.**

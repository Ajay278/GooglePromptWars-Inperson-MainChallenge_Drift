# DRIFT – AI Behavioral Drift Prevention System
### Product Requirements Document (v1.0)

---

# Product Vision

DRIFT is an AI-powered mobile-first web application that prevents harmful digital habits before they become addictions.

Instead of measuring screen time, DRIFT measures **Attention Drift**—the gradual shift from intentional behavior to unconscious digital habits.

Using Generative AI, behavioral science, psychology, emotional reinforcement, and human accountability, DRIFT helps users reclaim attention and build healthier real-world lives.

---

# Product Philosophy

> **The AI learns the person you're trying to become.**

> **Celebrate the life you reclaimed, not the minutes you saved.**

The goal is not to maximize engagement.

The goal is to help users eventually stop needing the app.

---

# Problem Statement

Current digital wellbeing apps:

- Measure screen time
- Show charts
- Block apps
- React after unhealthy habits form

They rarely explain **why** habits happen or how to replace them.

DRIFT focuses on prevention instead of punishment.

- Biggest distraction

- Healthy habits

## 2. Attention Drift Engine (USP)

Measures gradual behavioral drift.

Examples

Reading ↓

Exercise ↓

Sleep ↓

Phone Usage ↑

Mindless Unlocks ↑

Late-night Scrolling ↑

Outputs

- Drift Score
- AI explanation
- Predicted future risk

The AI always explains WHY.

## 5. Emotional Reward Engine

Reward life.

Not screen-time reduction.

Examples

Instead of

"You reduced screen time."

Say

"You reclaimed enough time to finish two chapters of your book."

Instead of

"7-day streak."

Say

"You protected seven distraction-free family dinners."

Every reward references the user's personal goals.

---

## 6. Human Accountability Loop

Optional trusted buddy.

Examples

- Friend
- Parent
- Partner
- Mentor

Buddy receives encouragement opportunities.

Never surveillance.

Never automatic sharing.

User always controls what is shared.

---

## 7. Weekly AI Reflection

AI generates a personalized summary.

Includes

- Wins
- Triggers
- Patterns
- Improvements
- Next focus

Narrative instead of charts.

---

## 8. Life Wishlist (Future Self Profile)

Users define the person they want to become.

Examples

- Read 20 books
- Get fit
- Spend more time with family
- Learn guitar
- Sleep better
- Build side projects

The AI references these aspirations in every coaching session.

Example

Instead of

"Stop scrolling."

Say

"You wanted to become someone who reads every evening. Would you like to read for 20 minutes instead?"

# Responsible AI

## Privacy

- User owns all data.
- Minimal data collection.
- User can delete all data.

## Transparency

The AI always explains WHY recommendations were made.

## User Control

Users decide

- Accept suggestion
- Ignore suggestion
- Share reflection
- Contact buddy

## Safety

Never

- Diagnose
- Manipulate
- Shame
- Create fear

If severe distress is detected, recommend professional support.

## Security

- Secure authentication
- Encryption in transit
- Encryption at rest
- Explicit consent before sharing

# Final Product Statement

DRIFT is an AI Behavioral Drift Prevention System.

It learns the person users want to become, detects unhealthy behavioral drift before habits become addictions, provides transparent AI coaching grounded in behavioral science and psychology, encourages meaningful offline experiences, celebrates life instead of screen-time reductions, protects user privacy, and gradually helps users become independent of the application itself.

**The ultimate success of DRIFT is when users no longer need DRIFT.**
